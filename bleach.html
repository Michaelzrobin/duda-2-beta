bleach

My cat is very clumsy. One day he knocked his cat food all over. As I was cleaning it with all purpose bleach spray I realized I had accidentally sprayed a blue c-print of mine. I stopped. The bleach was boiling the blue ink into a vibrant orange/red. I watched the poison breathe through out the colors. I wiped the bleach off with a paper towel, only to see that it had left that spot pure white as if there was never any image or ink there. I knew I had to bleach myself, I had to feel that fire across my skin. It had to bubble, flame and linger. Only then did I have a real chance at wiping myself clean. All of the images are color-prints treated with bleach.
